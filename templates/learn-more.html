    • How does it work ?

    When you connect to the Cisco Live wireless network, location information is calculated by Cisco Mobile Experience (CMX).

    Using the CMX API your distance is extracted and used to calculate how far you walk.

    How do I enter ?

    Simply register an account with your mobile phone that is connected to the Cisco Live wireless network.

    Once registered your name and distance travelled will appear on the leader board.
